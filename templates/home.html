About the Project:-

Customer Satisfaction Analysis: Based on a number of variables related to air travel, this research delves deeply into the understanding and forecasting of customer satisfaction. The principal aim of this project is to assess and improve customer satisfaction by identifying critical factors that influence the passenger experience through the application of machine learning techniques and data analysis.

Project Overview:-

A variety of factors, including age, flight distance, in-flight entertainment, baggage handling, cleanliness, arrival and departure delays, gender, customer type, travel type, and class type are all included in the information that we evaluate for this project. These characteristics are essential since they cover a wide range of aspects of a traveler's experience, from the caliber of in-flight amenities to the timeliness of departure times.

While flight distance and departure delay give contextual information about the route, age and gender offer demographic insights. The standard of services received throughout the flight is reflected in the following areas: cleanliness, baggage handling, and in-flight entertainment. Arrival delay affects the whole travel experience, which further reduces satisfaction. The characteristics of a passenger's travel and their degree of service are indicated by their customer type, travel type, and class type.

Technologies and Tools Used:-

To achieve the objectives of this project, several Python libraries are employed:

NumPy and Pandas: Used for data manipulation, cleaning, and preprocessing.

Matplotlib and Seaborn: Utilized for data visualization, helping to identify trends and patterns in the data.

Scikit-learn: Implements machine learning algorithms to build and evaluate predictive models.

Flask: Serves as the web framework to create an interactive web application interface, allowing users to input data and receive predictions.

Joblib: Used for saving and loading the trained models efficiently.

SQLite3: Manages the database for storing customer data and results.

This project's online interface is made utilizing HTML, which guarantees an approachable platform for user interaction with the model. By entering multiple data, including age, flight distance, and service quality ratings, users can use this tool to anticipate client satisfaction levels.

Impact and Utility:-

The ultimate objective of this initiative is to offer airlines practical insights to raise customer happiness and improve their services. Airlines may make well-informed judgments to maximize their services and resolve any potential concerns by knowing the key aspects driving passenger experience and forecasting satisfaction levels.

This project emphasizes the value of data-driven decision-making in improving consumer experiences in the travel sector, in addition to showcasing the real-world use of machine learning and data analysis.
